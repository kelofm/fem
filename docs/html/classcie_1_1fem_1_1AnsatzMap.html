Detailed Description

template<class TValue>
class cie::fem::AnsatzMap< TValue >

Utility class for matching ansatz functions on different boundaries to preserve continuity.

Example in 2D with ansatz functions generated from the outer product of the following set:

\[ \begin{align} \mathcal{N}_0(x) &= \frac{1 + x}{2} \\ \mathcal{N}_1(x) &= \frac{1 - x}{2} \\ \mathcal{N}_2(x) &= (1 + x) (1 - x) \end{align} \]

The resulting ansatz functions in 2D local space:

\[ [N_i(\xi,\eta)] = \frac{1}{4} \begin{bmatrix} (1+\xi) (1+\eta) \\ (1-\xi) (1+\eta) \\ 2 (1-\xi^2) (1+\eta) \\ (1+\xi) (1-\eta) \\ (1-\xi) (1-\eta) \\ 2 (1-\xi^2) (1-\eta) \\ 2 (1+\xi) (1-\eta^2) \\ 2 (1-\xi) (1-\eta^2) \\ 4 (1-\xi^2) (1-\eta^2) \end{bmatrix} \]
